Financial Transparency

How Your Contribution Helps

Your contributions directly support our campaigns to ensure safe, authentic, and adulteration-free food in Gujarat. As an NGO, we rely on the generosity of citizens like you to fund our outreach operations.

Rapid Adulteration Detection Kits

We assemble and distribute kits containing simple chemicals and instructions that help households test milk, ghee, honey, and spices for adulterants at home.

Public Outreach & School Seminars

Funding permits us to print educational brochures and hold live demonstration seminars in schools, colleges, housing societies, and village community halls.

Helpline & Legal Advisory Desk

Funding supports the administrative costs of running our helpline, allowing us to counsel citizens and guide them in filing formal complaints to food safety authorities.

"Divy Jyot Foundation is registered under the Gujarat Public Trusts Act, 1950. All contributions are utilized in compliance with NGO financial laws and accountability regulations."
