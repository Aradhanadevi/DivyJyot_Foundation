Have you purchased or consumed food that appears to be adulterated, stale, or contaminated? As a consumer, you have the legal right to safety and representation. Divy Jyot Foundation is here to support you at every stage of the grievance process.

How the Complaint Support Process Works:

  • Verify: Guidance on doing preliminary checks of the suspected food sample.
  • Report: Drafting and filing official complaints to FSSAI (FoSCoS portal) or Local Health Officers.
  • Escalate: Approaching district consumer courts in cases of severe contamination or health injuries.
