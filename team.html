Nitin Trivedi

Founder & Chairman

A Vision for an Adulteration-Free Gujarat

"Food is the foundation of life and health. Yet, food adulteration represents a silent crisis affecting millions of families in our state. Divy Jyot Foundation was established to empower the citizen, bridging the gap between consumers and regulatory enforcement."

Nitin Trivedi is a passionate public health advocate and social entrepreneur with over a decade of community organization experience in Gujarat. Realizing the lack of easily accessible rapid testing facilities and regulatory support for common households, he founded Divy Jyot Foundation to organize testing workshops, distribute detection kits, and provide a direct helpline for consumers.
