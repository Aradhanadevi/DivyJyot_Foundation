Together we can build an Adulteration free Gujarat 

Food Adulteration remains one of the most serious threats to public health. Millions of Consumers unknowingly purchase and consume food product that may contain harmful chemicals, artificial colors, unauthorized additives, contaminated ingredients, or misleading labeling.

Divy Jyot Foundation works to educate consumers, spread awareness about food safety, encourage reporting of food safety concerns, and promote responsible practices throughout the food supply chain.

About Divy Jyot Foundation

Divy Jyot Foundation is a non – Profit Organization committed to Protecting Consumers and Promoting Food safety awareness throughout Gujarat.

The Foundation Believes that safe food is a basic right and that every citizen deserves protections from adulterated and unsafe food Products.

Through education, advocacy and Community engagement, we work to create awareness about food safety and empower consumer to make informed choices.
